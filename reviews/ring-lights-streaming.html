Ring Light Size Guide

  • 10" ring lights: Good for video calls and selfies. Affordable entry point.
  • 12-14" ring lights: Versatile for streaming and content creation.
  • 18" ring lights: Best for full-body shots, makeup tutorials, and professional use.
Pro tip: Place your ring light directly in front of you, at eye level. The ring should reflect in your eyes as a circular catchlight—this creates the signature "ring light look" that's flattering for everyone.
